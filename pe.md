# PE Deals

Private equity teardowns focused on buyouts, growth equity, secondaries, and continuation vehicles. Each memo evaluates the asset like an LP and like a sponsor.

## Public memos

*Selected anonymized or methodology-only memos will be published here. Live confidential work is kept in a private archive.*

## Memo structure

Every PE memo follows the same structure:

1. Executive summary
2. Asset and business model
3. Industry and cycle position
4. Revenue quality and customer concentration
5. Margin profile and operating leverage
6. Capital structure and leverage capacity
7. Cash conversion and working capital
8. Management and incentives
9. Value creation plan
10. Exit paths and comparables
11. Returns model: base, upside, downside
12. Key risks and mitigants
13. PE scorecard
14. Diligence checklist
15. Recommendation

## Focus areas

- Lower and middle market buyouts
- Growth equity in software and healthcare
- PE secondaries and continuation funds
- Carve-outs and corporate divestitures
- Roll-up and platform strategies
