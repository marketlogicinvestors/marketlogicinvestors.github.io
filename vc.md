# VC Deals

Teardowns of venture-stage companies. Each memo is built like an investment committee deliverable: thesis, market, product, traction, unit economics, team, risks, scorecard, and recommendation.

## Public memos

*Selected anonymized or methodology-only memos will be published here. Live confidential work is kept in a private archive.*

## Memo structure

Every VC memo follows the same structure:

1. Executive summary
2. Company and product
3. Market and timing
4. Traction and clinical or commercial outcomes
5. Platform, data, and defensibility
6. Go-to-market and payer or channel strategy
7. Funding history and cap structure
8. Competitive landscape
9. Unit economics
10. Team
11. Risks and mitigants
12. VC scorecard
13. Diligence checklist
14. Bull and bear case
15. Recommendation

## Methodology

Memos combine primary research, founder and operator calls, PitchBook and Perplexity Premium data, payer and clinical literature, and structured scorecards. All public posts are anonymized or use only information that is already public.
