# Frameworks

Reusable analytical frameworks behind every memo and thesis. These are the templates and scoring systems used in Investments Labs.

## Available frameworks

- VC Scorecard: team, market, product, traction, moat, terms
- PE Scorecard: revenue quality, margin profile, leverage capacity, exit clarity, management
- Unit Economics Cheat Sheet: CAC, LTV, payback, contribution margin, cohort retention
- Payer and Channel Map: how to map a healthcare or vertical SaaS company across its payer or buyer base
- Competitive Positioning 2x2: how to place a company on price and differentiation
- Risk Heatmap: market, product, regulatory, financial, team, exit
- Diligence Checklist: written diligence questions grouped by clinical, commercial, financial, technical, legal
- Bull and Bear Construction: how to build a defensible two-sided case
- 5-Year Prediction Template: base, upside, downside scenarios with named milestones

## How to use

Frameworks are designed to be opinionated and quick to fill out. Each one will be published as a standalone post with the template, an example, and notes on common failure modes.
