# Contact

Market Logic Investors LLC runs Investments Labs as a venue for industry theses, VC and PE deal teardowns, and reusable analytical frameworks.

## Get in touch

- For founders raising a round and looking for written diligence-grade feedback
- For LPs and co-investors interested in collaborating on theses or deals
- For operators and analysts who want to contribute a guest memo or framework

Reach out through LinkedIn or email. Details available on request.

## What this site is not

- Not investment advice
- Not a solicitation
- Not a public archive of confidential client work

All confidential deal work is kept in a separate private archive. Only anonymized or methodology-only content is published here.
