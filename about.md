## Market Logic Investors LLC

Market Logic Investors LLC is the personal investment research vehicle of **Hemanth Reganti**.

Harvard Business School PLD (accelerated MBA track, alumnus July 2026), MIT alumnus, and Venture Partner with GoingVC. Active angel investor and advisor across AI, enterprise transformation, deep tech, and digital health.

## What This Site Is

A personal research archive. It contains:

- **Industry theses**: long-form views on categories, refreshed quarterly
- **VC deal teardowns**: anonymized case studies and public-company analyses
- **PE deal teardowns**: analysis built from public filings of announced transactions
- **Frameworks**: the templates and decision rubrics behind the memos

## What This Site Is Not

- Not investment advice
- Not a solicitation
- Not a fund
- Not a place where live confidential deals are discussed

All case studies are anonymized or derived from public information. Confidential deal work is kept in a private archive, never published here.

## Methodology

Every memo follows the same structure: a canonical deal memo template, a scorecard, a risk heatmap, and a diligence question bank. Three input streams: public data, primary research, and inferred analysis. Numbers are tagged with their source on first appearance.
